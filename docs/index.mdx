skcal tracks weigh-ins, body measurements, and nutrition, and gives you a daily
snapshot of where you stand against your targets. It's built to be driven by
software: a typed HTTP API, a CLI, and a remote MCP server so your agents can
log and query your data.

## What you can do

- **Log** weigh-ins (with optional body-fat % and notes), body measurements, and meals.
- **Estimate meal macros** from a photo or a plain-text description.
- **Track** calories and protein against daily targets, and weight against a goal.
- **Ask the agent** for a blunt read on what to eat, grounded in your real numbers.
